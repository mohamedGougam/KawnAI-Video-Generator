# Kawn sample prompt library

Short, high-signal prompts tuned for the built-in **style** presets. Mix and edit freely.

## Cinematic

- Golden hour drone shot over a coastal city, soft lens flares, gentle waves, subtle Kawn orange light trails in the sky.
- Slow dolly through a rain-soaked neon alley, reflections on wet asphalt, shallow depth of field, moody grade.

## Realistic

- Locker room scene before a match: jerseys hanging, chalk dust in the air, handheld documentary camera, natural stadium light through windows.
- Close-up of training shoes on turf, sweat and grass stains, late afternoon sun, photoreal detail.

## Animation

- Stylized 3D mascots celebrating a goal, exaggerated motion smear, bright team colors with black outlines, stadium confetti burst.

## Social media reel

- Fast-paced vertical montage: kickoff, crowd wave, slow-motion splash of water bottle, punchy transitions, high energy beat-synced motion.

## Sports

- A cinematic sunset over a football stadium with cheering fans, orange and black Kawn branding on LED boards, energetic atmosphere, flying confetti.
- POV sprint down the sideline as a winger cuts inside, motion blur on legs, roaring crowd bokeh.

## Nature

- Misty mountain ridge at dawn, sun rays through clouds, a lone runner silhouette on a trail, calm and inspirational tone.

## Futuristic

- Holographic tactics board floating above a smart pitch, particles of light, sleek black UI chrome with orange accent lines, sci-fi stadium exterior.

## Brand-forward (use with any style)

- “Shot on cinema camera” look, **Kawn orange (#FF6B00) and deep black** as hero colors, premium sports-tech vibe, no readable sponsor logos.

Remember: keep descriptions specific about **camera**, **lighting**, and **motion** — short videos read better when the model knows what should move.
